**Global Tech & AI News: Top 5 Stories in the Last 24 Hours**

Here's a summary of the top 5 global tech and AI news stories from the past 24 hours:

1.  **Apple Unveils iOS 18 with Extensive AI Features**
    Apple has announced a significant update to its iPhone operating system, iOS 18, which will incorporate a wide range of artificial intelligence-powered features. These updates aim to enhance Siri's capabilities, offer AI-driven tools for users, and improve personalization options. New features include the ability to create custom emojis ("Genmojis"), schedule text messages, and enhanced AI for managing notifications and summarizing content. Some advanced AI functionalities will be exclusive to the iPhone 15 line due to processor requirements.

2.  **Nvidia Reports Record Earnings Driven by Soaring AI Chip Demand**
    Nvidia has announced another quarter of record-breaking earnings, largely propelled by the immense demand for its AI processors. The company's revenue surged, with its data center segment showing significant growth. Nvidia's CEO expressed confidence in continued strong demand for their latest AI systems, noting that sales for their Blackwell-generation platforms have been exceptionally high.

3.  **Microsoft Enhances Azure with New AI Foundry Platform**
    Microsoft is bolstering its cloud offerings with the introduction of Azure AI Foundry, a unified platform designed to simplify the development, customization, and management of AI solutions. This new platform integrates with existing developer tools like GitHub and Visual Studio, aiming to bridge the gap between AI technologies and practical business applications. Azure AI Foundry will also offer insights for IT professionals and business leaders to track AI initiative effectiveness and align them with business goals.

Surging demand for AI-enabled technologies is driving innovation in chip design, with investment and market orders accelerating the transition from research to mass production. Chinese chipmakers are demonstrating adaptability, with many companies posting profits and leading in revenue growth within the digital chip design and manufacturing sectors.

    Google DeepMind continues to push the boundaries of AI research, announcing breakthroughs in areas such as advanced reasoning, multimodal understanding, and efficiency with its latest Gemini models.
